Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

ChargeShield ("we", "our", or "us") is a Shopify application that helps merchants automatically collect evidence and respond to payment disputes and chargebacks. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

When you install ChargeShield on your Shopify store, we collect and process the following data through the Shopify API:

  • Store information (shop domain, plan, timezone)
  • Order details related to disputed transactions (line items, shipping address, billing address, order total)
  • Customer information associated with disputed orders (name, email address, order history)
  • Fulfillment and tracking information (carrier, tracking number, delivery status)
  • Shopify Payments dispute information (dispute ID, reason code, evidence due date, amounts)
  • Fraud and risk assessment data provided by Shopify

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data collected solely for the following purposes:

  • Automatically collecting and organizing evidence for payment disputes
  • Generating dispute response packets on behalf of merchants
  • Tracking dispute deadlines and sending reminder notifications
  • Displaying dispute status and evidence within the ChargeShield dashboard

We do not sell, rent, or share your data or your customers' data with any third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. Data Retention

Dispute evidence data is retained for 24 months following the resolution of a dispute, after which it is permanently deleted. You may request early deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

4. Data Security

All data is stored in a SOC 2-compliant cloud database (Supabase/PostgreSQL) with encryption at rest and in transit. Access to personal data is restricted to authorized systems and logged via our audit trail. We maintain a security incident response policy and will notify affected merchants promptly in the event of a breach.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell customer data. We may share data with the following sub-processors solely to provide the service:

  • Supabase – database hosting and storage
  • Resend – transactional email notifications to merchants

6. Your Rights

As a merchant using ChargeShield, you may at any time:

  • Request a copy of all data we hold about your store
  • Request deletion of your store's data
  • Uninstall the app, which will trigger deletion of your session and access tokens

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@chargeshield.app.

7. Shopify's Role

ChargeShield accesses your store's data through the Shopify API in accordance with Shopify's Partner Program Agreement and API Terms of Service. We request only the minimum scopes required to provide the service.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify merchants of material changes via email or in-app notification. Continued use of ChargeShield after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, reach us at privacy@chargeshield.app.
